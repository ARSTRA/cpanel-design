Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Gun-k Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, make purchases, or engage with our services related to firearms and accessories retail.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when:
  • Creating an account or registering on our website
  • Making a purchase or placing an order
  • Completing ATF Form 4473 for background checks
  • Requesting information about our products or services
  • Subscribing to our newsletter or marketing communications
  • Participating in surveys, contests, or promotions
  • Contacting customer support

1.2 Types of Personal Information

The personal information we may collect includes:
  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, Social Security Number (for background checks)
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, mailing address, billing address
  • Government IDs: Driver's license, state ID, passport (for age verification and legal compliance)
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing information (processed securely)
  • Legal Information: FFL license numbers, residency status, background check responses
  • Purchase History: Transaction records, product preferences, order history

1.3 Automatic Information Collection

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:
  • IP address and location information
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website information
  • Search terms used on our website

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:
  • Legal Compliance: Conducting federally required background checks through NICS
  • Order Processing: Processing purchases, managing inventory, and fulfilling orders
  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your customer account
  • Customer Service: Responding to inquiries and providing support
  • Marketing: Sending promotional materials and product updates (with your consent)
  • Security: Protecting against fraud and ensuring website security
  • Analytics: Improving our website and services through usage analysis
  • Record Keeping: Maintaining legally required records per ATF regulations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:
  • Legal Requirements: With law enforcement, ATF, or other government agencies as required by law
  • Background Checks: With the National Instant Criminal Background Check System (NICS)
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in business operations
  • FFL Dealers: With licensed firearms dealers for transfer purposes
  • Payment Processors: With secure payment processing companies
  • Legal Protection: When necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:
  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with firewall protection
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal information
  • Secure storage of physical documents per ATF requirements
  • Regular data backups with encryption

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for different periods depending on the type of information:
  • ATF Records: Permanently retained as required by federal law
  • Transaction Records: Minimum of 20 years per ATF regulations
  • Account Information: Until account deletion or as legally required
  • Marketing Data: Until you opt-out or request deletion
  • Website Analytics: Typically 2-3 years for business analysis

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion where legally permissible
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing
Note: Some information cannot be deleted due to federal firearms regulations and legal requirements for maintaining ATF records.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience:
  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand website usage
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Deliver targeted advertisements
You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

10. State-Specific Rights

10.1 California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information (subject to legal exceptions)
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

11. International Users

Our services are primarily intended for U.S. residents. International users should be aware that their information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where privacy laws may differ from their home country.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
